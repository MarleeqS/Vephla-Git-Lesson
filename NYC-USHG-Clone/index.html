Restaurants & Bars

From neighborhood cafes and vibrant fine dining, to waterfront taco
stands and local watering holes, Union Square Hospitality Group has
created some of New York's most beloved restaurants and bars.

Blue Smoke brings mouthwatering barbecue, sides,
and a Southern-inspired beverage menu to Battery
Park City.

Ci Siamo brings live-fire cooking, housemade
pasta, and a robust Italian wine list to the heart of
Manhattan West.

Ci Siamo →

Your neighborhood kitchen from morning, noon, to
night, Daily Provisions serves an all-day menu that
fits the many needs of our hungry neighbors, with
coffee, crullers, and b.e.c’s in the morning, made
-to-order sandwiches, salads, and seasonal soups
in the afternoon, and a dinner menu inclusive of
roast chicken, classic sides, and an array of bakery
favorites.

Gramercy Tavern has been serving up American

Gramercy Tavern →
